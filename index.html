Coiled Soul Studio

Living sculptures
with a soul

Hand-built figures in stoneware, coil by coil — each one made to hold a living plant, and to mark a moment worth remembering.

View the work

Julie Kiefer has spent most of her life making living art — sewing clothes for her three kids, stitching quilts to keep family cosy, planting an English garden in the backyard. Making has always been her way of caring for others.

With Coiled Soul Studio, Julie hand-builds sculptures using the coiling technique, shaping each into a figure meant to hold a living plant. Many of her pieces mark something: a birth, a loss, a season of life worth remembering. The result feels personal and alive — because it is.

Where figurative clay meets living plants.
